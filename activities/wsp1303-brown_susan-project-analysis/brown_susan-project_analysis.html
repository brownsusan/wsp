Competitive Analysis

Casa Guacamole has positive and competitive ratings on third party websites such as a 5 star rating on VRBO. As far as information architecture, the site's competitors have a more organized and logical flow of content. In addition, to remain competitive with similar sites, the code needs to be made more semantic to improve search results ratings and provide better accessibility. Another way that the code could be improved upon would be implementing the separation of structure and style.

Casa guacamole is also not using any form of keywords or meta data in their site. Most of their competitors are using meta data in order to enhance their web presence.
